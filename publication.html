The Journey Of The Comb

Date：2023/02
Catagory：Publication
The comb, as a private and intimate item, seems to be limited in our imagination in daily life, often only treating it as a 'comb'. When we re examine it, each comb carries an independent emotional narrative. The journal of the Comb analyzes the respondents' information and the comb length, width, comb tooth spacing/angle used. It records the unique language produced by the five interviewees themselves and the comb, reshaping the comb as an activator of a new dialogue form. This language seems to come from the poetry that the comb praises when it combines with people, attempting to explore the deeper meaning of the "comb" item to individuals in different dimensions.

The Guide to The Good Life

Date：2022/11
Catagory：Publication
A guide book for RAM Highlights 2022:
During this difficult year, participants asked "what is a good life", and the designer responded through the typefaces and graphic design. English capital letters tightly wrapping the lowercase letters inside, Chinese 好 and 活 it is closed in 美 and 生. The two sets of typeface designs bring a lot of associations. The graphics are derived from the green, yellow and red health codes, and the graphics look like a common tablecloth or woven bag from a distance.The guide was designed as an additional graphic material for the exhibition.
