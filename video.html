I, Selfie Company

Date：2023/06
Catagory：Video
In the digital age, we leave our digital footprint through the use of various application software and digital platforms. Like 3D printing technology, we showcase the virtual self created by numbers to each other. This behavior is like turning life into data. I question whether being dominated by data is a behavior of losing oneself. How can I find my own position?
I have created an algorithmic controlled personalized human identity archive company, I Selfie Company, where humans can use cutting-edge artificial intelligence algorithms to reproduce their digital selves, including personality traits and unique quirks. By creating their own digital copies, users can preserve their image and personality for future generations, leaving behind a lasting legacy that they can be proud of.

No Tag On Me

Date：2022/08
Catagory：Video
Collaborative project with Adidas：
Must we live as defined by others? What exactly is beauty like? Young souls also need various forms. Every girl is unique and beautiful, imperfect and different, which makes us truly who we are. The film tells the story of five girls bound by bars, who, under the influence of adidas girls, accept their imperfections and regain their confidence.

The girls in the short film all have different personalities, including a bold and confident clover girl, a girl with deep body anxiety, a girl troubled by weight loss, a girl born darker than others, a girl who is not confident due to birthmarks, and a girl who feels inferior due to her big mouth and freckles These characters may be both you and me, and we don't need to live in the eyes of others.
